Collecting data – simplified

Storing and receiving data is extremely easy with MedicQ. Once a patient has completed a questionnaire, all you have to do is log back in with your password and then choose to send the results back via Wi-Fi or USB. Security is one of our main priorities, so we encrypt your data with the powerful AES algorithm to ensure your data is safe at all times.

Powerful desktop companion

MedicQ works best with its powerful desktop companion. It’s robust, efficient, and really easy to use.
